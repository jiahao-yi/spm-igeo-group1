# Supplier Decision Draft

## Candidate Suppliers

- FunIOT: start-up company with lower cost, agile process, and promising IoT experience.
- ProfCar: long-established company with higher cost, stronger process maturity, certifications, and more smart car experience.

## Candidate Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- |
| Cost | The project budget must not exceed 1,000,000 euros. |
| IoT and smart car experience | iGEO depends on IoMT features and integrations with external devices and cloud services. |
| Safety and security reputation | TOMI stakeholders are concerned about safety, privacy, and public reputation. |
| Process maturity | Clear processes are important because requirements and deployment can become difficult. |
| After-sales support and warranty | Hardware and display components need reliable support after delivery. |
